About Us

We produce & market a wide range of fresh and frozen dim sum – traditional Chinese delicious delicacies, pastries, shrimp & meat dumpling and spring roll, which are divided into fried, steam, baked and microwave categories. Different delicious varieties are available such as vegetable , seafood, and chicken meat etc. filling and ingredients can be adjusted to suit the market’s requirements. Most importanly, All our products are Halal Certified and MSG free !
